Smart Attendance LMS

Digital Attendance & Academic Management Platform
Prepared for: MLA Academy of Higher Learning Date: 23 February 2026 Document: Commercial Proposal

Overview

Smart Attendance LMS is a digital platform that automates attendance management, academic reporting, and parent communication. The system is designed for use across all departments and streams within the institution, accessible on any device without requiring application installation.

Capabilities

  • Subject-wise digital attendance marking
  • Multi-stream and semester management
  • Language elective and combined class support
  • Attendance reports with percentage breakdowns
  • Student records management
  • Faculty management with subject assignments
  • Semester-end promotion with rollback
  • Mentor-student relationship tracking
  • Administrative dashboard with live statistics
  • Intelligent query assistant (natural language)
  • DLT-registered SMS alerts for parents
  • Push notifications for staff
  • Installable as mobile app (no app store required)
  • Role-based access with secure authentication
